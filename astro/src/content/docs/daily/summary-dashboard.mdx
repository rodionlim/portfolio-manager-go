The Summary Dashboard is the fastest way to answer, "Where is my portfolio exposed right now?" It groups your current positions into practical views and lets you jump into the underlying holdings when something needs attention.

Open it from the sidebar:

```text
Summary
```

## What It Shows

At the top, Portfolio Manager shows portfolio-level totals in your base currency:

| Metric | What it means | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Market Value | Current total value after FX conversion | `SGD 482,400` |
| Overall P&L | Total unrealized profit or loss after FX conversion | `SGD 38,250` |
| Day Change | Change from cached previous prices, when available | `SGD -1,280 (-0.27%)` |

Day Change appears only when cached historical prices are available for your current tickers.

## Allocation Tables

The dashboard then breaks the portfolio into four tables:

| Table | Use it to answer |
| --- | --- |
| By Book | Which strategy, account, or portfolio sleeve holds the value? |
| By Currency | How much exposure sits in each trading currency? |
| By Category | Which sectors or categories dominate the portfolio? |
| By Asset Subclass | How much is in stocks, ETFs, govies, cash-like assets, or other subclasses? |

Each row shows value and P&L. When day-change data is available, rows also show the day's movement. Percentages beside amounts show each row's share of that table's total.

## Useful Actions

- Click a Book row to open Positions filtered to that book.
- Expand a Category row to inspect the tickers contributing to that category.
- Use the pencil action in expanded category details to update missing or incorrect reference data.
- Sort Value, P&L, or Day columns when you want to find the largest exposure or biggest mover quickly.

## Before You Rely on the Numbers

Check these inputs when a number looks wrong:

- Positions come from your imported or manually entered blotter trades.
- Currency conversion uses each position's FX rate.
- Category and asset subclass come from reference data, with Singapore government securities grouped as Govies.
- Day Change needs cached previous prices. If it is missing, run or schedule historical price collection.
